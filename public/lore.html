The Origin of the Mashup

In the quiet cycles of the digital Mushroom Kingdom, a singularity occurred. It wasn't a virus, nor a bug. It was an evolution—a spontaneous reorganization of the world's underlying logic. The Mashup Engine was born from this chaos.

The tiles shifted. The clouds became bricks. The ground turned to logic. We didn't build the Mashup; we merely learned to speak its language.

The Alpha Breach

Early explorers report that the boundaries between worlds have thinned. A Goomba in World 1-1 might now possess the tactical awareness of a Boss Fortress guardian. Pipes lead to unmapped sectors. Coins are no longer just currency; they are the fuel for the AI module's reality-forge.

The Role of the Hero

Units like MARIO_PROTO and LUIGI_GHOST are our only way to stabilize these sectors. By navigating the generated reality, they provide the feedback loop needed for the Gemini Module to refine the kingdom's architecture.

Future Projections
